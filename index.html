The real problem

AI is everywhere. Control is not.

Most firms already use AI across people, tools, documents, workflows, vendors, and software platforms. The dangerous part is not usage. The dangerous part is invisibility.

AI inside operations

  • Employees using ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, Claude
  • AI-generated reports, offers, emails, and documents
  • AI-assisted HR, support, marketing, analytics
  • AI embedded inside CRM, ERP, SaaS, and vendors

FAQ

Questions firm owners ask first.

Does every company using AI fall under the EU AI Act?

Most firms using AI in the EU are likely in scope. The first step is understanding operational AI exposure.

What is the first thing I should do?

Build an AI inventory. Visibility first. Risk classification second. Obligations third.

What is shadow AI?

Employees or teams using AI tools without formal approval, inventory, oversight, or governance.

Can AiVenture certify compliance?

No. We support readiness and implementation. We do not provide legal advice, certification, or notified body services.
