FAQ

Questions answered here.

Is this legal advice?

No. This is operational readiness support, not legal advice, certification, or notified body service.

Is the Start Free path really free?

Yes. The starter path gives access to initial visibility, inventory, and readiness orientation.

What is the first operational step?

Build visibility: identify where AI is already used, by whom, with what data, and for what purpose.

Who is this for?

Firm owners, SMEs, marketing agencies, media teams, IT departments, consultants, and non-EU AI providers.

What does Done For You mean?

AiVenture structures the inventory, evidence, governance workflows, and machine-readable trust layer.

Do I need an AI inventory?

Yes. Without inventory you cannot reliably classify risk, assign ownership, or organize evidence.

Can AiVenture certify compliance?

No. AiVenture does not certify compliance and is not a notified body.

What can be automated?

Discovery, inventory updates, metadata, logs, reminders, and some evidence workflows can be automated.

What remains human?

Risk judgement, accountability, human oversight, policy decisions, and legal interpretation remain human responsibilities.

How does this connect to multichannel production?

Each section can become website content, LinkedIn posts, KDP chapters, Udemy lessons, YouTube scripts, chatbot intents, and AI voice flows.
